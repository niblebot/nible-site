Lesson 1

It is very important that you observer the customs of the Free when you enter one of the Houses.
Protocol dictates that a two tone doorbell be fitted to the premises, and that upon arrival the first tone sounds until its harmonics have completely diminished.  Then and only then will the button be released to signal entry to the premises.  The Occupier will open the door to the premises in synchronisation with the Visitor.   This is at Phileas' command and begins the process.

The customary greeting at this point is "Ha Ma Free" (hah-mah-free), meaning "Hello My Friend"
