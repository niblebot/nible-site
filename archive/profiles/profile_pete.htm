Born: 12th July 1999
You can call him: Peb
Species: DNA reconstructed indult (enhanced cross between an adult and an infant)
Likes: vodky, tiny hairs on cold water molecules, Jamiroquai, the whiteness of white paper.
Dislikes: fit women, demonic possession, chunky food, the letter W.
Often disguised as: Tall hats.

Profile: This is the newest era of human species, he has been generated by Dr Timeslice's spawned engineering practices, and the work of many scientists before him. Nicknamed Pebulus Rhomtide after the famous philosopher Pebulus Plato, and the intrepid explorer Regina Rhomtide, he has the entire history of the earth stored in his cranial abdomen. Although Dr Timesilce testified that Pebulus had now flaws, there was an article printed in The Daily Nine about 9 years ago that the positioning of the cranial abdomen was too straight in relation to the pulmonary aorta, so the functional specification was hindered by 0.9%. This lead to the Seriously Secret Organization (S.S.O) to conduct an investigation into the findings of the paper. Although there was no evidence found, the publicity which this generated contributed to the downfall and eventually suicide of Dr Timeslice at the Rhombic Laboratories. Pebulus however managed to escape and is now in asylum with the Horsonian Society who generously donated an island to Pebulus for habitation. They now communicate via the Parasky Internet since Pebulus does not come into contact with anyone anymore.
