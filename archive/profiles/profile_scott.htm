Species: Eclectic bass phenomenon size Snigger

Dislikes: groovy shoes, noisy neighbors, dust, natural light.
Often disguised as: Red Rum.

Profile: He is one of the oldest people on the Earth today. Born as a result of genetic breeding, he is capable of crying out load the likes of 10 times louder than a speeding bullet. After founding the Horsonian Society for the appreciation of horses, he went on to genetically enhance them hence the term nowadays 'A Jaeger horse is for life'. Literally, the horses he bred have an average lifetime of 999 years, beyond any species of beings. He spent a lot of time in the so called 'innerspace' of horses, discovering that a new source of power resided in the sub-structural gradient of the molecular decimation of purple blood cells resident only in the adrenal gland in horses. After this he found Edward Yu a lost soul in need of redemption and proceeded to dedicate some of his time to Ed's recovery. He taught him the ways of philosophizing and eventually accepted him as a conciliatory member of the Horsonian Society. Scott not only saved this precious soul, he also gave him the name of Tamskyu. Scott can now be found residing in the Topaki Islands off the western coast of Africa where he spends the majority of his time perfecting the ultimate Volvo Horse, hopefully creating the ultimate warriors stead.
