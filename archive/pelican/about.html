Pelican Quality Technology Resources is a company supplying IT and Telecommunications contract and permanent personnel.

We work in partnership with our clients and registered personnel to fully and accurately analyse their respective requirements. We endeavour to meet these requirements by taking time to fully understand the job specification from the client and the type and nature of the role being sought by the prospective candidate.

We supply permanent and contract staff across all skill sets from Project Managers, Network Technicians, Analyst Programmers, IT Support staff, and many more, to Software Developers, Help Desk personnel and Computer Operators.

We currently deal with banks and other financial institutions, utilities, local government departments, the legal profession and most major manufacturers.

We are aware that this is a highly competitive business area but we think that our particular approach, together with our considerable experience of the IT world, allows us to provide a level of service which raises us above the competition.

Pelican.QTR aim to be first choice, upholding our stated intentions of supplying high quality personnel in conjunction with an accurate assessment of the job skills required.
