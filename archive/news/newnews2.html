Woman in Boxing Accident

 Today a woman was found in an abandoned apartment as the police raided the location for suspected drugs packages. Instead this horrific accident was found in the kitchen. No explanation was necessary as to the cause of the accident. By the side of the box was the latest can opener available on the market, this was the sole cause of the accident. It had been let go temporarily, and a power surge switched it on and then it hastily cut up the unfortunate woman. The mystery is who put the woman into the box. Hopefully further DNA sampling will reveal the suspect. Early indications point towards the neighbours pet cat.
