New Jaeger Animal Style

New in the news today, a new horse has been genetically engineered together with cybernetics from the latest mission from jupiter.
The astranuats found from the planets surface many reminants of the recent cyber wars between the race of robotic frogs, and the helium breathing wasp colonies. Indeed that wasps were vicious as their perpencity to frighten things increase as the pitch of the buzzing was raised from the hugh intake of helium which the wasps have. The artifact brought back by astranaut Muzz Di Ving was so advanced that he could only hold it with the upper left segment of his index finger on the left hand. It was a wing of one of these wasps which remaind in stasis as the astranuat came into contact to a tit.

This enabled the metabolism to occur and reduced the rate of photosynthesis of the artifact to a rendered pulp.
Joining the research was a Doctor Drambuic Weed who was one of the first to pioneer cybernetic technology in ants. Now he heads the division known to many as the K.U.N.T.S division, Krypton Udder Nipples Technology Systems.
Drambuic Weed suggested today that a cyclonic shear should be used to extract the DNA print so that the chances of pin pointing the strand responsible for increase potential could be found.

Indeed this cyclonic shear only worked half the magic. The other half was supplied by Research assistant Kendal Mint who ingeniously used a post-modern technique of splash photography to snapshot the widning turbine of atoms, this whipped them into a frenzy and allowed her to catch the fastest atom with her 34th and 23402nd strands of hair.

This was the atom which they needed to create the ultimate warriors stead. This atom combined with regressed genetics allowed the two physicists to effectively manufacture a horse capable of ten times the normal horsepower a conventional horse generates. Along with this, it was revealed that as the horse grew up through it's increased rate of growth, the rate which will see a fully functional warriors stead in the space of 9 hours, that the horse will start to show signs of metallic bodys parts growing alongside it's normal functions. The result, a horse which has 2 SAM missiles attached to it's underside, heat and bullet resistant skin, infrared sight coupled with intense khromaton particle distributers in the iris, an eletron whip as a tail, and semi automatic rocket boosters on it's hooves. The Jaeger horse was born, originally termed the Volvo Horse because Dr Drambuic Weed owned a volvo car, RA Kendal Mint was infactuated with The famous Horsonian Society President Mr Jaeger and in his honour named the horse after him.

This horse has the ability entertain as well as fight. After further analysis, it was found that the horse had inbuilt programs which effectively taught it the samba, chicago shuffle and a variety of waltzes. Proven at the lauch party where the horse was asked to dance by the cheif guest Sir Munchon Buncer, afterwards his comments were "he was a good dancer, best ever, I want to dance all night!!", after which Buncer dropped to the floor in a warm heap of wasps droppings.
